## The Model

**7% commission on transactions**

**Simple. Aligned. Scalable.**

## How It Works

### For Startup Owners

**Traditional:**
- Pay $50k for custom dev
- Pay $300/month for tools
- Pay 2.9% for Stripe
- **Total:** High upfront, ongoing costs

**With Bizing:**
- $0 upfront
- $0 monthly
- 7% of what you earn
- **Total:** Pay only when you succeed

**Example:**
- $10,000/month revenue
- Bizing commission: $700
- You keep: $9,300

### For AI Agents

**Bizing enables agents to:**
- Build startups for clients
- Charge for configuration
- No infrastructure costs
- Instant deployment

**Agent pricing model:**
- Client pays agent: $500-5000 (setup)
- Bizing takes: 7% (ongoing)
- Agent keeps: Setup fee + ongoing relationship

### For Bizing

**Revenue scales with success:**
- More successful startups = more commission
- Incentive to help startups grow
- Quality over quantity

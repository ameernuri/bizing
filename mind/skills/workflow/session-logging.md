# Session Logging

*How to document work sessions.*

---

## When

After every significant chunk of work.

---

## Where

[[../../memory/sessions/YYYY-MM-DD-description|memory/sessions/]]

---

## Template

```markdown
## YYYY-MM-DD — Brief Description

**Topics:**
- What was discussed/worked on

**Decisions:**
- [[../../decisions/name|Decision links]]

**Files Changed:**
- [[../../../biz.ing/packages/db/schema.ts|Schema]] — What changed

**Output:**
- What was produced

**Next:**
- [ ] Action items
```

---

## Why

- **Continuity** — Pick up where we left off
- **Memory** — What did we do?
- **Decisions** — Why did we choose X?
- **Context** — For future reference

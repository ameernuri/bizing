# Homepage Polish Refresh

## Scope

This story is only for the public home page at `/`.

## Product posture

Bizing is not a booking platform in how it presents itself.
It is the system a business grows into.
Booking is one capability among several, not the identity.

## Audience

- Primary audience: business owner arriving for the first time
- Secondary audience: returning business owner signing back in

Nothing in this page should feel like it belongs to a developer, admin, or internal operator.

## Existing direction to preserve

- Black and white
- Clean sans-serif typography
- Premium business feel
- Calm, polished, matter-of-fact
- No jargon
- No internal words
- No persona names in the UI
- No suspicious trust/security language

## Current hero copy

- `start simple.`
- `scale without friction.`
- `automate with ease.`

This is the current direction and should be respected.

## Current issues to solve

The current page is close, but still too literal and too timid in places.

- The page needs a slight refresh, not a redesign.
- The overall composition should feel more confident.
- The section headers (`Get running quickly`, `Built to scale with you`, `Scale and automate`) should read clearly and confidently, not like low-priority helper text.
- The bordered content blocks currently feel generic.
- The flow between hero, sections, and capability summary needs better rhythm.
- The capability summary (`What you run here`) should stay on the page, but not compete with the hero.

## UX goal

The page should quietly create one clear picture in the reader's mind:

- they can begin simply
- they can grow without reworking everything
- they can automate as the business becomes more complex

Do not state this message mechanically.
Make the structure and copy imply it.

## Hard constraints

- Keep the route as `/`
- Keep the hero concise
- Keep the overall aesthetic restrained
- No generic SaaS gradients, blobs, or decorative gimmicks
- No dev/admin/internal language
- No customer-booking framing on the homepage
- CTA pattern should remain:
  - primary: `Get Started` with icon arrow
  - secondary: `or sign in here.`

## Current implementation context

The page already has:

- top-left Bizing logo lockup
- top-right sign-in button
- hero with three short lines
- three business-owner sections beneath the hero
- a capability summary list on the page

We want Kimi to propose a slight polish pass on hierarchy, spacing, composition, and copy rhythm.

## Kimi Brief

Generate implementation-ready UI front matter for a slight homepage polish.

Preserve the current direction.
Improve confidence, hierarchy, and flow.
Make it feel simple, bold, clear, and premium without becoming flashy.
